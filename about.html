About Jonathan Edwards

With a solid foundation in mechanical engineering, I have honed my skills in problem-solving, analytical thinking, and practical application. My journey has been marked by a commitment to excellence, whether it’s optimizing mechanical systems or diving deep into the intricacies of engineering design.

Embracing the challenges of coding and development, I've been exploring new technologies, programming languages, and development methodologies. As I continue to evolve in both fields, I am driven by the desire to integrate engineering principles with cutting-edge software innovations. Whether working on a complex mechanical project or crafting the next big software solution, my goal is to push the boundaries of what’s possible and make a meaningful impact in the tech world.
