FAQs - Graph Plotter

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Graph Plotter?

The Graph Plotter is a web-based tool that allows users to input data and generate a line graph based on the values provided.

How do I use the Graph Plotter?

To use the Graph Plotter, enter your X and Y values in the input fields provided. You can also label the X and Y axes and give your graph a title. Click "Plot Graph" to generate the graph based on your inputs.

Can I download the graph I create?

Yes, after generating the graph, you will see a "Download Graph" button. Click this button to download your graph as a PNG image.

What should I do if the graph doesn't display correctly?

If the graph doesn't display correctly, make sure your X and Y values are correctly formatted and have the same number of elements. If the problem persists, try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache.

Who can I contact for support?

If you're experiencing issues or have questions, you can contact the developer.
